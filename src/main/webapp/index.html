ConnectU



ConnectU is a platform for the students all over the world. Here, everyone can connect themselves with others from same academic subject background.
Knowledge is soo diverse that it's impossible for one individual to absorb everything. You need to collaboate with other people to learn and grow. ConnectU provides you the platform to find people of same subject interests and and you can have a chance to explore more about your interests alongwith peers around the world.
