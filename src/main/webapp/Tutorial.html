Tutorial



Login Page:

  • You need to login in order to start chatting with peers.
  • Click on the Login in navigation bar.
  • In login page enter your email.


Your Page:

  • You can add information about yourself in the about me section and then clicking Submit button.
  • You can add messages in the message box.
  • The button bellow that says "hide negative messages" and "show negative messages" can help you to hide or show the negative messages respectively.
  • In the message section when you will add url of any image you can see its image at the place of url as well. (Try using url: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/3a/Cat03.jpg)
  • In the bellow section you can also upload any image.


Public Feed:

  • You can click on Public Feed at navigation bar to see all the messages.
  • The button bellow that says "hide negative messages" and "show negative messages" can help you to hide or show the negative messages respectively.


About Our Team:

  • You can click on about our Team at navigation bar to see about us.
  • Alternatively you can scroll down the main page to see about us.
  • When you bring your curser on our pictures you can see information about us.
  • To know about our Project advisor click on Project Advisor in dropdown of About our team in navigation bar.


Map:

  • You can click on Map at navigation bar to see your location.
  • This map can assist you to see your nearby location where ever you are.
  • May be you would like to search by yourself from which country you peers belong to.
